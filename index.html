OUR VISION

Family Restoration Ministries and Services, Inc's vision is to see and experience fully restored, emotionally stable and developed families, couples and individuals of our communities.

Committed to provide individuals, families and couples with training and mental health services.

OUR SERVICES

This International Ministry exists to fulfill a threefold purpose:

  • The worship of God in Spirit and Truth (John 4:23).
  • Walk not after the flesh, but after the Spirit (Romans 8:4)
  • The edification and encouragement of the body of believers (Ephesians 4:16, Hebrews 10:24-25).
  • The fulfilment of the Great Commission to make disciples of all nations (Matthew 28:19).

WHAT WE DO

Family Restoration Ministries and Services, Inc is committed to provide individuals, families and couples with training and mental health services that will focus on a holistic approach that covers the spiritual, physical, mental, emotional and social conditions of persons of all ages. We are experienced in assisting individuals to adjust and adapt to their environment, achieve emotional stability and regain healthier relationship connections by providing them with the necessary tools to accomplish success.

Our Values

  • We are Christian Based

  • Honesty and integrity
  • To be loyal and obedient to those we serve as we are to God, Jesus and the Holy Spirit.
